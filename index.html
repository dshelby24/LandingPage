What is Selfie City?

Selfie City Cincy is a photo experience company that provides artistic studio space. Our studio made up of 6 exhibits filled with custom installations and hand painted murals by local Cincinnati artists.


What we do at Selfie City?

Selfie City holds many events from pop-up shops, sip and paints and many more. Selfie City also caters to the millennials who snaps and captures every moment, book Selfie City for a girls night out or business warming party!

Do I have to be a photographer to book?

Absoulety not! Selfie City Cincy is a great place for everyone including podcasters, videographers, photographers, etc. This is an expierence you dont want to miss.
